Made using simulation models

STEPWAT2 simulates how climate change and disturbance regimes affect dryland plant communities by modeling soil water availability and plant competition. It considers individual responses to changing conditions, aiding in understanding demographic and community-level shifts.

Use STEPWAT2

Supporting literature

Dryland ecosystems are vulnerable to climate change and disturbance. STEPWAT2's individual-based approach helps quantify these impacts, particularly in big sagebrush communities, and helps assess climate change effects and inform mitigation strategies for dryland ecosystems.
