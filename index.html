The STEPWAT2 App is an free online tool that quickly visualizes and analyzes vegetation data for the Western United States. Examine potential trends for points or selected regions for big sagebrush, perennial and annual grasses, and forbs. Download data from specific datasets and regions for further analysis.

Launch app

Made using simulation models

The App uses data from the STEPWAT2 model, which utilizes a process-based soil water model (SOILWAT2) to simulate available soil water in multiple soil layers, which plant individuals compete for based on the temporal matching of water and active root distributions with depth.
